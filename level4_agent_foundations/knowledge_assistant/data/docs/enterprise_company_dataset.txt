Internal Knowledge Base – Apexion Technologies (Enterprise)
1. Company Overview

Apexion Technologies is a global enterprise specializing in cloud computing, AI-driven analytics, and large-scale distributed systems. The company operates across North America, Europe, and Asia, serving enterprise clients in finance, healthcare, and retail.

2. Work Policy & Hours
Standard working hours: 9 AM – 5 PM (local timezone)
Flexible work arrangements available depending on team requirements
Employees must attend all mandatory team meetings
3. Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to:

20 days of paid annual leave
12 days of sick leave
5 days of emergency leave

Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to 10 days.

Leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 3 days in advance.

4. Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
Fully remote roles require manager and HR approval
Employees must maintain availability during core hours
5. IT Security Policy
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long
Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory
Passwords must be updated every 90 days
Sharing credentials is strictly prohibited

Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour.

6. Data Privacy & Compliance

Employees must:

Handle customer data securely
Follow GDPR and internal compliance standards
Avoid unauthorized data sharing

Violation of data policies may result in termination.

7. Internal Tools & Platforms

Apexion uses:

Jira → task management
Slack → communication
Confluence → documentation
GitHub → code repositories
8. Onboarding Process

New employees must:

Complete HR documentation
Set up company email and accounts
Attend onboarding sessions
Gain access to required tools
9. Expense Reimbursement Policy

Employees can claim:

travel expenses
accommodation
business meals

Claims must be submitted within 7 days with receipts.

10. Performance Evaluation

Employees are evaluated:

every 6 months
based on KPIs, impact, and collaboration

Managers provide feedback and growth plans.

11. Code of Conduct

Employees must:

maintain professionalism
respect diversity and inclusion
avoid conflicts of interest

Harassment or misconduct leads to disciplinary action.

12. Incident Reporting

Employees must report:

security breaches
policy violations
system failures

Reports can be submitted anonymously via the internal portal.
